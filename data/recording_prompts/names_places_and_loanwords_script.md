# Names, Places, and Loanwords Recording Script

Use these prompts to record names, place references, and everyday loanword-heavy speech in Comorian. Keep one prompt per audio file when possible, and speak naturally and clearly.

## Personal Names

1. My name is Omar.
2. Her name is Fatima.
3. His name is Ali.
4. I saw Amina at the market.
5. Yusuf called me this morning.
6. Mariam is coming with us.
7. Hassan is waiting outside.
8. Khadija brought the food.
9. Abdallah went to the mosque.
10. Salma is my sister.
11. Hamza is my younger brother.
12. I met Ibrahim yesterday.
13. Zainab lives near the road.
14. Musa is a good teacher.
15. Halima is at home today.

## Family and Community Names in Sentences

1. Omar and Fatima went to the market.
2. Ali came with his brother Yusuf.
3. Mariam greeted Khadija after prayer.
4. Hassan and Abdallah are working together.
5. Amina called Salma in the evening.
6. Ibrahim visited Musa at school.
7. Zainab and Halima cooked together.
8. Hamza is playing with Ali outside.
9. Fatima asked Mariam for help.
10. Yusuf and Omar arrived early.

## Place Names

1. I am going to Moroni today.
2. She came from Mutsamudu yesterday.
3. We traveled to Fomboni last week.
4. He lives in Mitsamiouli.
5. They are visiting Iconi this afternoon.
6. I have family in Domoni.
7. The meeting is in Moroni.
8. The boat is leaving for Anjouan.
9. My uncle works in Mohéli.
10. She returned from Grande Comore.
11. We visited a friend in Mayotte.
12. The road to Mitsoudje is busy today.
13. He bought fish near the port in Moroni.
14. They moved from one village to another.
15. Our relatives live near the coast.

## Local Place Descriptions

1. The mosque is near the school.
2. The market is beside the main road.
3. The clinic is behind the bus stop.
4. The port is crowded this morning.
5. The road to the village is narrow.
6. We met near the beach at sunset.
7. The children are playing near the well.
8. The farm is up the hill.
9. There is a shop near the mosque.
10. Our house is close to the market.

## Common French Loanwords

1. I am using the telephone.
2. The doctor is at the hospital.
3. She works in an office.
4. The director called a meeting.
5. I need the document today.
6. He brought the paper and the pen.
7. The student forgot the notebook.
8. The car stopped near the market.
9. We are waiting for the bus.
10. She opened the radio in the morning.
11. I charged my phone last night.
12. The school has a new teacher.
13. He repaired the motor yesterday.
14. The machine is not working.
15. I went to the station early.

## Loanwords in Daily Sentences

1. The doctor told me to rest at home.
2. My phone battery is low.
3. The bus arrived late this morning.
4. He lost an important document.
5. The radio is too loud.
6. She wrote her name with a blue pen.
7. The car needs fuel.
8. The teacher explained the lesson clearly.
9. I called him on the telephone.
10. The office is closed today.
11. The student arrived without a notebook.
12. The machine broke during work.
13. I heard the news on the radio.
14. The driver stopped near the station.
15. The hospital is far from our village.

## Mixed Names, Places, and Loanwords

1. Omar called Ali from Moroni.
2. Fatima went to the hospital in town.
3. Yusuf took the bus to the market.
4. Amina heard the news on the radio.
5. Hassan brought the document to the office.
6. Mariam met Khadija near the port.
7. Ibrahim called Salma on the telephone.
8. Musa drove the car to Mitsamiouli.
9. Halima saw Abdallah at the station.
10. Zainab went to school in the morning.
