# English Recording Script for Comorian Speech Collection

Use these English prompts as source material to translate into Comorian and record as single-speaker audio clips. Keep each recording to one prompt per file when possible.

## Greetings and Introductions

1. Peace be upon you.
2. Good morning, how are you today?
3. Good evening, it is nice to see you.
4. My name is Omar.
5. What is your name?
6. I am happy to meet you.
7. Welcome to our home.
8. Please come in and sit down.
9. Thank you for coming today.
10. I have not seen you for a long time.
11. Where are you coming from?
12. I am from this village.
13. This is my friend.
14. Let me introduce my sister.
15. We are glad you are here.
16. How is your family doing?
17. Please greet everyone for me.
18. I hope you arrived safely.
19. It was good talking with you.
20. Goodbye, we will meet again tomorrow.

## Numbers, Dates, Time

1. I need one cup of tea.
2. There are two children outside.
3. She bought three mangoes at the market.
4. We arrived at four o'clock.
5. He has five books in his bag.
6. Today is the sixth day of the month.
7. My meeting starts at seven in the morning.
8. They waited for eight minutes.
9. I paid nine thousand francs.
10. We finished the work at ten at night.
11. Yesterday was Monday.
12. Tomorrow will be Friday.
13. The date is the fifteenth of June.
14. What time is it now?
15. It is half past three.
16. I will return in twenty minutes.
17. She was born in the year two thousand.
18. We stayed there for two weeks.
19. The bus leaves early in the morning.
20. The shop closes late in the evening.

## Family and Relationships

1. My mother is cooking in the kitchen.
2. My father is working outside.
3. I have one older brother and two younger sisters.
4. This is my grandmother's house.
5. Our uncle lives in another town.
6. My cousin is visiting us this weekend.
7. Her husband is a teacher.
8. His wife works at the clinic.
9. Their baby is sleeping quietly.
10. My son likes to play football.
11. My daughter is reading a story.
12. We are a close family.
13. My parents taught me to respect people.
14. She is my best friend from childhood.
15. He is like a brother to me.
16. We gathered for a family meal.
17. My aunt helped me when I was sick.
18. Their grandfather tells stories every night.
19. I miss my relatives who live far away.
20. Family support is very important to us.

## Food and Shopping

1. I am hungry and I want to eat now.
2. Please bring some rice and fish.
3. We bought fresh bananas at the market.
4. The bread is still warm.
5. I need salt, oil, and onions.
6. How much does this cost?
7. That price is too high for me.
8. Can you give me a lower price?
9. I want one kilo of tomatoes.
10. She is cooking beans for lunch.
11. The tea is too hot to drink.
12. We ate together after sunset.
13. I would like to buy new clothes.
14. The market is very busy today.
15. He sells fruit near the road.
16. Please put the food in a clean bag.
17. I forgot to buy sugar.
18. This meat smells fresh.
19. We shared the meal with our neighbors.
20. I will come back tomorrow to shop again.

## Health and Body

1. I do not feel well today.
2. My head hurts.
3. She has a fever and needs rest.
4. His stomach is hurting after the meal.
5. Please take the child to the clinic.
6. I need medicine for this cough.
7. Drink more water and rest at home.
8. My back feels sore.
9. He injured his leg while working.
10. The nurse checked my temperature.
11. I have pain in my chest.
12. Her eyes are red from dust.
13. Wash your hands before eating.
14. The doctor said I should sleep early.
15. My body feels tired today.
16. She is getting better slowly.
17. We must keep the room clean.
18. Healthy food gives the body strength.
19. He went to the hospital in the morning.
20. Please tell me where the nearest clinic is.

## Directions and Places

1. Where is the nearest mosque?
2. The school is behind the market.
3. Turn left at the big tree.
4. Walk straight until you see the bridge.
5. My house is near the main road.
6. The shop is across from the bus stop.
7. We are going to the beach this afternoon.
8. He lives on the other side of the island.
9. The hospital is far from here.
10. Please show me the way to the port.
11. The road to the village is narrow.
12. We met near the well.
13. The children are playing in the yard.
14. I left my bag at home.
15. She works in the town center.
16. The meeting will be held at the community hall.
17. Our farm is up the hill.
18. There is a small shop beside the mosque.
19. We sat under a tree near the river.
20. This path becomes muddy when it rains.

## Work and School

1. I am going to work early today.
2. She teaches mathematics at the school.
3. The students are taking an exam.
4. He forgot his notebook at home.
5. Our teacher asked us to read aloud.
6. I need to finish this report before noon.
7. The children arrived late for class.
8. We are learning new words today.
9. My brother works as a driver.
10. She opened her shop at sunrise.
11. The manager called a meeting this morning.
12. I want to improve my skills.
13. The lesson was difficult but useful.
14. He is writing on the board.
15. We studied together after school.
16. Please submit your work on time.
17. The office is closed on Sunday.
18. She wants to become a nurse.
19. Hard work can change a person's life.
20. Education is important for every child.

## Religion and Community Phrases

1. Peace and mercy of God be upon you.
2. May God bless your family.
3. We are going to the mosque for prayer.
4. The imam spoke to the community after prayer.
5. We gathered to help our neighbor.
6. Thank God for this new day.
7. If God wills, we will meet tomorrow.
8. Please remember us in your prayers.
9. The community worked together to clean the road.
10. We shared food after the gathering.
11. Let us welcome the visitors with kindness.
12. The elders called for patience and respect.
13. We visited a sick neighbor in the evening.
14. Charity helps people in need.
15. Everyone came together for the celebration.
16. The children are learning verses at school.
17. We should speak to each other with respect.
18. May peace remain in this community.
19. The meeting started with a short prayer.
20. Our people become stronger when we help one another.

## Questions and Answers

1. Where are you going?
2. I am going to the market.
3. Did you eat this morning?
4. Yes, I ate before leaving home.
5. Who called you last night?
6. My cousin called me after dinner.
7. Why are you late today?
8. The road was blocked by traffic.
9. When will you come back?
10. I will come back before sunset.
11. Which house do they live in?
12. They live in the blue house near the road.
13. Can you help me carry this bag?
14. Yes, I can help you now.
15. Do you know this person?
16. No, I have never met him before.
17. What happened here yesterday?
18. There was a meeting in the village.
19. Is the shop still open?
20. No, it closed an hour ago.

## Common Commands and Requests

1. Please sit here.
2. Open the door slowly.
3. Close the window before you leave.
4. Bring me a glass of water.
5. Wait for me outside.
6. Speak clearly so I can understand you.
7. Write your name on the paper.
8. Call me when you arrive.
9. Help the child cross the road.
10. Give this message to your father.
11. Do not touch the fire.
12. Please clean the table after eating.
13. Turn off the light.
14. Come back quickly.
15. Show me what you bought.
16. Tell them to come now.
17. Listen carefully to these instructions.
18. Stop here for a moment.
19. Please repeat what you said.
20. Let us leave before it gets dark.

## Storytelling and Daily Life Descriptions

1. Every morning I wake up before sunrise.
2. I sweep the yard and prepare tea.
3. The children get ready for school.
4. After breakfast, I go to the market.
5. On the way, I greet my neighbors.
6. The market becomes crowded very early.
7. I buy vegetables, rice, and cooking oil.
8. In the afternoon, the weather becomes hot.
9. We rest for a short time after lunch.
10. Later, I visit a friend in the next village.
11. She tells me about her day.
12. We laugh and share stories together.
13. In the evening, everyone returns home.
14. The children play outside before dinner.
15. My grandmother tells a story after we eat.
16. Sometimes the electricity goes out at night.
17. When it rains, the roads become slippery.
18. On weekends, we visit family members.
19. Life here can be hard, but people help each other.
20. At the end of the day, I thank God and go to sleep.
