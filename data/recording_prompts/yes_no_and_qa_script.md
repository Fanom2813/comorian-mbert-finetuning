# Yes/No and Question-Answer Recording Script

Use these prompts to record short conversational speech in Comorian. Keep one prompt per audio file when possible, and speak naturally as if answering or asking in real life.

## Yes and No Responses

1. Yes.
2. No.
3. Yes, I understand.
4. No, I do not understand.
5. Yes, I am coming.
6. No, I am not coming.
7. Yes, that is correct.
8. No, that is not correct.
9. Yes, I saw him.
10. No, I did not see him.
11. Yes, I have eaten.
12. No, I have not eaten yet.
13. Yes, she is at home.
14. No, she is not at home.
15. Yes, we finished the work.
16. No, we have not finished yet.
17. Yes, I remember.
18. No, I forgot.
19. Yes, please.
20. No, thank you.

## Confirmation and Clarification

1. Is that true?
2. Yes, that is true.
3. No, that is not true.
4. Did you say tomorrow?
5. Yes, I said tomorrow.
6. Did you mean this house?
7. No, I meant the next house.
8. Can you repeat that?
9. Please speak more slowly.
10. I did not hear you well.
11. Say that again, please.
12. Is this the right place?
13. Yes, this is the right place.
14. No, this is the wrong place.
15. Did you understand me?
16. Yes, I understood you.
17. No, I did not understand.
18. Let me explain again.
19. I want to make sure.
20. Now I understand clearly.

## Everyday Question-Answer Drills

1. Did you eat? Yes, I ate.
2. Are you coming? No, I am not coming.
3. Where is he? He is at home.
4. What do you want? I want water.
5. Who called you? My sister called me.
6. When will you return? I will return tonight.
7. Why are you waiting? I am waiting for the bus.
8. How is your father? He is doing well.
9. Did she arrive? Yes, she arrived early.
10. Is the shop open? No, it is closed.
11. Are the children sleeping? Yes, they are sleeping.
12. What happened? The car broke down.
13. Where did you go? I went to the market.
14. Do you know him? No, I do not know him.
15. Can you help me? Yes, I can help you.
16. Did it rain yesterday? Yes, it rained a lot.
17. What did you buy? I bought rice and sugar.
18. Who is outside? Our neighbor is outside.
19. When does school start? School starts at seven.
20. Why is she crying? She is in pain.
